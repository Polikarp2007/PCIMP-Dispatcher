M200 Arad - Radna

Developed by Poli&Co S.R.L. and released in March 2026, this route spans approximately 40 km. 
It is located in the Crișana region of Romania and forms part of the Pan-European Corridor IV, featuring a high-speed connection towards Hungary. 
Designed to perfection, it stands as the most technologically advanced and intelligent route ever created.

The route is officially licensed, fully integrated, and configured for multiplayer (PC|MP) by the PC|MP developers in collaboration with the publisher, Poli&Co. 
It is optimized for both dispatchers and train drivers within the PC|MP environment.

The route features 6 stations: 
- Arad 
– Glogovăț 
– Ghioroc 
– Păuliș hc. 
– Păuliș 
– Radna. 

Dispatchers enjoy full, realistic control over all stations, with the sole exception of Păuliș hc.

Key features include:

Complete Signaling & Switch Control: Fully synchronized between all players in real-time.

Integrated Communication: Built-in voice and text chat to connect with all players.

Dedicated Dispatch Zones: Custom-designed areas tailored for each dispatch post.
